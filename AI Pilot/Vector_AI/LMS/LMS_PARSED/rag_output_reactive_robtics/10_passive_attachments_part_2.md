Class 8: Passive Attachments - One Way Door

 

The passive attachment we are going to build next is called a one way door (or a one way switch).

![One way door](https://dp4rp4tcpuoo1.cloudfront.net/R600V2/640/owd.png) The action of this device allows something to pass in but not out.  Watch the video below to see how this works. A great place to use a one way door is when a mission requires something to be pulled.

[Video: One Way Door](https://player.vimeo.com/video/952769680)


Now that you've seen a demonstration of the one-way door, it's time to build your own! The key to success is ensuring that your pivot connection—the swinging component—is secured with a gray or tan frictionless pin, allowing it to move smoothly. Additionally, you'll need a stopper to prevent it from swinging in the opposite direction.
