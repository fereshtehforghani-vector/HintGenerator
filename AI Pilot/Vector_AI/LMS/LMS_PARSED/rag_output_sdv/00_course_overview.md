**Hello and welcome to the Self-Driving Vehicle course!**

If this is your first course with **Zebra Robotics**, we’re excited to have you on board—welcome to the Zebra family!

In this course, you'll dive into the fascinating world of autonomous vehicles. You’ll learn how to **design, build, and program a self-driving car** using **sensors, cameras, and microcontrollers**. This stream focuses on a blend of **electronics, mechanical design, and coding**, giving you hands-on experience in building a car that can think for itself.

Your coach will be there every step of the way—to guide you, answer your questions, and review your work.

**Stream Sequence:**


- **Design Chassis with Lego **

- 
**Introduction to ZBot** – Getting started with ZebraBot****


- 
**Coding Concepts (C++)** – Learn the language of automation

- 
**Motors ** – Control motion with precision

- **Integrate motors with Lego Chassis**

- 
**CAD Design Basics** – Learn to design in 3D

- 
**Car Design** – Build chassis, steering, and gearbox

- **Integrate motors with 3D printed Chassis**

- 
**Working with Sensors** – Distance, colour, and gyroscopic sensors

- 
**Using a Camera** – Vision for autonomy

 

**Course Learning Outcomes**


- 

Interpret circuit diagrams and understand the flow of current and voltage


- 

Apply digital electronics concepts and control circuits using code


- 

Design 3D models using CAD software


- 

Use sensors to gather data and respond intelligently


- 

Build and program your **autonomous car**
